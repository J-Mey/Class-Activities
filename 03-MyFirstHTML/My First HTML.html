  2. Grab a few slices of meat of your choice
  3. Grab cheese if desired or any other items such as lettuce, onions, tomatoes etc.
  4. Place meat, cheese, lettuce, onions and tomatoes in between the two slices of bread
  5. Your sandwich is complete, now enjoy!
